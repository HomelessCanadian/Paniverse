Malfouf Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette Recipe Servings: 4-6 | Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Total Time: 15 minutes Description: A classic Lebanese salad made with shredded cabbage, fresh herbs, feta cheese, and a tangy lemon vinaigrette. Ingredients: 1 head of cabbage, shredded 1/2 cup of chopped fresh parsley 1/2 cup of chopped fresh mint 1/4 cup of chopped fresh dill 1/4 cup of chopped scallions (green onions) 1/4 cup of crumbled feta cheese 1/4 cup of chopped walnuts 2 tablespoons of lemon juice 1 tablespoon of olive oil 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard 1/2 teaspoon of honey Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: In a large bowl, combine the shredded cabbage, chopped parsley, mint, dill, scallions, and feta cheese. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, Dijon mustard, and honey until smooth. Pour the vinaigrette over the cabbage mixture and toss to combine. Sprinkle the chopped walnuts over the top of the salad and serve. Lemon Vinaigrette Recipe: 2 tablespoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice 1 tablespoon of olive oil 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard 1/2 teaspoon of honey Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, Dijon mustard, and honey until smooth. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Store the vinaigrette in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
